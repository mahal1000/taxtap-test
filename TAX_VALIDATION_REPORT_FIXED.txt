Manual review notes
- This build remains a sole-trader style estimator for standard England/Wales/Northern Ireland income-tax bands plus Class 4 NIC.
- It is not a full accountant-grade engine for every special case (for example Scottish bands, marriage allowance transfers, student loan, child benefit charge, dividend regimes, CIS, capital allowances, basis-period edge cases, payments on account, or VAT logic).
